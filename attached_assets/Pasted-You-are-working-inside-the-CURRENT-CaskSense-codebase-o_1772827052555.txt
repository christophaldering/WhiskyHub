PRODUCT INTENTION
======================================================================

Historical tasting data comes from a SPECIFIC real-world whisky community and should NOT be fully transparent to every user by default.

The desired product logic is:

A) PUBLIC / GENERAL LEVEL
General users may see aggregated, anonymized, non-sensitive historical insights such as:
- top-performing whiskies in historical blind tastings
- average score patterns
- region/cask/smoky trends
- generalized “group taste profile” style insights

But they should NOT automatically see:
- full historical tasting archives
- specific historical lineups
- detailed tasting-by-tasting rankings of that community
- community-identifying or participant-identifying data unless explicitly allowed

B) COMMUNITY LEVEL
Users who are assigned to the relevant community may see the detailed historical archive for that community, including:
- historical tastings
- lineups
- rankings
- tasting detail pages
- richer insights tied to that community

C) PRIVATE / ADMIN LEVEL
Admin/internal users should be able to:
- inspect the imported historical data
- inspect visibility assignments
- inspect unmatched/low-confidence imports if that module exists
- manage community linkage where appropriate

The model should be extensible for future multiple communities, not hardcoded for exactly one.
